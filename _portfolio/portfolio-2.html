--- title: "Project 2" excerpt: "Experiments to examine the growth of E. coli while changing the carbon source.
" collection: portfolio --- I conducted experiments demonstrating that transitioning the carbon source from glucose to galactonate induces a diauxic growth curve when cultivated in minimal media. This observation underscores the necessity of a diauxic period for the production of enzymes and other cellular machinery crucial for metabolizing galactonate as the primary carbon source.
